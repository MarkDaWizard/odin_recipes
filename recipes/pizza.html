Pizza

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • Warm water
    • Dry Yeast
    • Cold water
    • Salt
    • Flour
    • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Toppings
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Dried oregano
    • Ground black pepper
    • Canned crushed tomatoes
    • Extra-virgin olive oil
    • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead Dough
  3. Form dough balls
  4. Make the pizza
  5. Preheat oven
  6. Form pizza
  7. Add toppings
  8. Put in oven
  9. Bake till brown
  10. Remove from oven
  11. Serve
